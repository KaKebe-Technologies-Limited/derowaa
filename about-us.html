Something About Us

DEROWAA EVENTS COMPANY LIMITED is an events management company that is aimed at also stretching out to a troubled world so as to connect the dots between the church and the outside world. We carry out events planning and management for events like conference shows, parties, wedding reception and also hire out equipment like tents, chairs, public address system, stage and stage lights and also offer services like outside catering, tour etc.

We organize music and dance competitions, conferences, science and cultural exhibitions and by this we have always offered the youth and other gifted people a platform to grow and become the best in what they can be. Through these gatherings we always inform, educate and entertain our audience in different aspects of life and promoting culture and traditions amongst the young generations of today!

Mega Talent Search

The event is called The Shadow of Greatness Mega Talent Search organized with other partnering organizations and it's a platform for the person’s to showcase and perfect the different gifts/skills in them. It shall also help promote talent among the people in the community in relation to technology, music, dance, etc and other exhibitions. The event is a gathering that shall bring together different categories of youth from different backgrounds, we are hoping to use it as a platform to connect the dots between the church and our troubled world with great people with talents. The Shadow of Greatness Mega Talent Search is to be held twice a year with the first being in April and the second is December every year as shall be organized by Derowaa Events. On 30th April this year, we are organizing the Second Edition of Derowaa Events at Lira Hotel.


The Champion of Change Conference

The Seven Seeds 2nd Annual Champion of Change Conference Themed Creativity, Innovation and Entrepreneurship for a better tomorrow. Today’s youth are tomorrow’s drivers of entrepreneurial activity in their communities, regions and countries and an increasing number of governments worldwide recognize the importance of investing in an entrepreneurship culture among young people, a crucial foundation for any sustainable and dynamic private sector. How can education systems, innovative ideas and the private sector most effectively promote entrepreneurial attitudes and talent in youth? In order to address this question,

The Champion of Change Conference is envisioned to develop and nurture youths to be creative, innovative and have good entrepreneurial skills for a better living tomorrow with a targeted number of youths key leaders in attendance. The Conference’s diverse and dynamic group of speakers, panelists, youths, education officials and practitioners provided an in-depth insight and refreshing wealth of perspectives, which will lead to new approaches in fostering entrepreneurial youth within the Lango Region and the surrounding regions.
