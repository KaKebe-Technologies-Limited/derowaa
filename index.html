About Derowaa Events

DEROWAA EVENTS COMPANY, is birthed from The Seven Seeds Uganda as a project designed and aimed at stretching out to a troubled world with the vision to transform the people of Northern Uganda into individuals who really do appreciate the art of creativity, innovation and entrepreneurship for a better tomorrow with culture. Derowaa Events stands out to promote talents of young people and promote culture in the regions of Uganda. The united bodies plan to organize The Seven Seeds 2nd Champion Of Change Conference and The Shadow of Greatness Mega Talent Search on 30th April 2022
